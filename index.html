Scenic Floats

Scenery seekers and photographers will enjoy a leisurely float through the Teton Wilderness. This is a unique opportunity to view wildlife in their natural habitat on the banks of the Snake River. Departure times vary with the earliest departure time at 6:00 AM and the latest at 5:30 PM. Trip times are approximately three hours to and from the meeting area in Grand Teton National Park.

Wilson to South Park

Another beautiful stretch of river on the Snake is from the Wilson Bridge to South Park, just south of Grand Teton National Park. You will meet in the town of Jackson for a short drive to your launch site. You have the option to stop at a private campsite for lunch or join, The FIRST LIGHT tour, the number one selling scenic trip when bird and wildlife activity is at its peak!
